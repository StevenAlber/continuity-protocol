What you get when you order your protocol

Not a generic checklist. Not a PDF template. A crisis continuity protocol built by hand for your family's exact situation, location, and risk profile. Delivered to your email within 24 hours.

Inside your protocol

After payment, you fill a short intake form about your family, location, passports, health, and concerns. We analyze your situation and build a document covering:

  • Your exit map — primary and backup routes from your current location, ranked by reliability and speed
  • Your timing logic — when to monitor, when to prepare, when to move. Not panic, but thresholds.
  • Your transport options — flights, ferries, land routes, transit hubs. What's realistic for your family.
  • Your document readiness — passport validity, visa requirements for your destinations, gaps to close now
  • Your financial continuity — which payment methods work where, backup access, cash recommendations
  • Your medication and health plan — refill windows, what to carry, where to source abroad
  • Your children's protocol — what to pack, how to explain, comfort kit, documentation they need
  • Your family communication plan — who to contact first, fallback channels, check-in protocol
  • Your fallback branches — if Plan A closes, here is Plan B. If B fails, here is C.
  • Confidence labels — every recommendation marked as Confirmed, Likely, or Emerging
